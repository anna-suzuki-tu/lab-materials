DeSFE Lab Education Portal

Institute of Fluid Science, Tohoku University — Anna Suzuki Laboratory
We value how you grow through research more than the research outcomes themselves. — DeSFE Lab’s educational philosophy

This is the portal for the educational materials we actually use at DeSFE Lab. It serves our lab members, and at the same time it shares with the outside world how we teach and design research. Students and educators are welcome to use these materials in classes and seminars.
