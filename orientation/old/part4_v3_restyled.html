④ Individual Meeting

Identity Alignment Space

Confirm research axis 研究軸確認
Growth direction & Career design 成長方向確認 / キャリア設計
Not for: Technical details or minor manuscript edits.
技術判断や原稿の細部修正には使わない。
Part 4 | Slide 5

Decisions vs Sparring

判断の場と、壁打ちの場

Idea (0→A)
Framing (A→B)
Figures (A→B→C)
Draft (Writing)
Submission 🎉
Team Meeting = Decision (GO/NO-GO) 判断：次のフェーズに進むか戻るかを決める。
Lab Forum = Sparring 壁打ち：問い・立場・表現を壊して磨く。
Part 4 | Slide 6

⑤ Individual Work

The Only Place Research Moves

Research doesn't move in meetings. It moves in your desk. 研究はミーティングで進まない。個人作業で進む。
Required Habits:
  • Daily Research Log
  • Weekly Paper Card
  • Monthly Core Question
Meetings Work
Meeting is for alignment.
Work is for progress.
